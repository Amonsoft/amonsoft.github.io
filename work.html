AMONSOFT WORK TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  1. We shall have to work 5days a week
  2. Everyone will be availed varying transport costs per month
  3. Internet consumption will be used sparingly to save costs
  4. Working time will be at least office contact 4hrs a day for everyone
  5. Everyone will need to have access to the communication channels for better communication which are:-
    • Slack-for messaging
    • Asana - for project management
    • Amonsoftinc.blogspot.ug
    • Amonsoft.github.io
    • Facebook.com/amonsoftgroup
    • Twitter.com/amon_ny
    • amonsoftx@gmail.com
    • And others to come
  6. Any one will be required to represent the company at corporate meetings, negotiations, duties and parties upon request
  7. Cooperation is encouraged for development and learning, so we shall be having a few meeting when necessary, and attendance is key to success.
  8. We expect everyone to learn something from the web engineering team at least once a week, for technology purposes
  9. The company will do weekly and monthly reviews to see effectiveness in terms of turning work into incomes.
  10. Remember the company's goal beneath everything else is to make money so that we can all have a better better better life and set an example, so let's cooperate.
NOTE: Failure to meet these conditions then the company cannot see a way in which it can develop and grow and generate revenue to meet its goals and everyone who works with it.
