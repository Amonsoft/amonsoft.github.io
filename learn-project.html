Overview of the learn Project

We have developed a program where we shall be able to empower younger people with different skills in technology development. Technology has really changed our understanding, our lives, what we like and how we behave in these century.The problem is that the people who can develop these technologies are really few and some dont have the neccesary skills and only have surface information.
