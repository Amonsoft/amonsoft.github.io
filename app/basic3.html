  • Rainbow Payment Service

    Rainbow payment system is a pin payment system that is development to close the gap between today’s realistic payment methods and the web. Payments have suffered a big blow online given the fact that USSD payments have succeed but offline. Ecommerce has grown to enormous rates in recent years sees more than 100Million worth of goods advertised in Uganda alone. So we have to change from 100 Million advertised to 100Million sold online. The current payment systems online use PayPal, Visa and MasterCard among others, but which are not readily available in our country. The issue is majorly to avail the demand of online purchasers with a proper medium of payment and ecommerce.
