End of Year Communication from Chairman Amonsoft Corporation

31, December, 2015

I would like to congratulate everyone for having reached to the end of 2015, so happy new year in 2016. The company Amonsoft has operated in 2015 which has turned out to be one of its pinnacle years. The company has achieved a lot and also has had its pit falls, but thank God we have made it to the end of 2015.

As we enter the New Year. We hope for more growth as a company, increase in revenue base, more implementation of projects, cutting unnecessary cost, developing better products and a lot more. We as Amonsoft have a responsibility to develop technology within our means to the furthest to be of importance in our communities' lives.

We have seen the company recruit many brilliant people from management personnel to software engineers and mechanical engineers all to contribute and enable build the company. Am delighted to say that in 2016, we hope to double our current revenue and reduce our expenditure to revenue ratio and as result we hope to be rolling out our first pay checks this year to our talented members.

I would like to encourage everyone working at Amonsoft saying that; the future is only for us to make, and so let's put our minds and hands to work and build products for which the world will reward us happily, so don't tire innovating for the company, work smart, work together, always be ready to learn, teach others, always speak out your mind and let your voice be heard, for at Amonsoft, we don't minimise any contribution, for we know even a dead clock is right twice every day.

The company is in a re-invention phase, this is where we are trying to rediscover who exactly we are, and our future is at stake here. The financials of the company, taxes, shares and other financial information is being reassessed to enable the company work better and faster.

Remember, this Company was built to offer a cool work place for people in the field of technology, with great innovations, great pay, for people to contribute to the technology sphere of not just our country but the world at large, let's keep it that way. As Amonsoft Corporation, we have set out to tackle Communication technology i.e. Smartphones, The Mobile Web, Virtual Reality and Transportation in 2016, we hope to pull it off, and we hope to start from where we left off in 2015.

Happy holidays and happy new year 2016, let's build the next billion Dollar technology company in Africa.

Nyesigye Amon
CEO and Chairman Board of Directors
Amonsoft Corporation
